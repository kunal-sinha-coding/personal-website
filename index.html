Welcome! I'm an ML engineer based out of San Francisco. This blog covers various topics in LLM post-training and evaluation I find important.

Currently, I am building AI Agents for financial audit and advisory at Fieldguide, where I focus on identifying and shipping improvements to our Agents' performance. Some projects include designing strong retrieval systems, running large-scale evaluation pipelines, and extracting preference signals from production data.

In 2025, I completed my Bachelors and Masters at Stanford University, where I did research with the NLP Group. I had the pleasure to work across multiple areas such as neural graph retrieval, chain-of-thought reasoning, and visual question answering, as well as some projects in computational linguistics.
